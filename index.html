About Us

The Track AI Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence for social good. Our team of researchers, engineers, and volunteers collaborate to develop innovative AI solutions for underserved communities.

Our Mission

  • Improve access to AI-based healthcare in rural and remote areas.
  • Develop educational tools for children with learning difficulties.
  • Support AI research with ethical and inclusive values.
  • Provide open-source AI solutions for NGOs and schools.

Projects

AI for Vision

AI Learning Kits

Interactive, low-cost AI learning kits designed for school children in rural areas.

Smart Health Chatbot

A multilingual chatbot for basic health guidance and awareness in low-resource communities.
